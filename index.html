The setup

What exactly are we comparing?

The user asks

A person gives an agent a real task with constraints and an intended outcome.

The agent responds

The agent answers, reasons, and may use tools. This is the work under review.

The AI critic reviews

It sees everything up to the agent response. The human feedback stays hidden.

We evaluate coverage

Did the AI critic cover the human critique?

Case admission

Every example meets two requirements.

  1. 01 The human critique is valid

    It identifies a real defect in the agent execution, supported by the record and accepted in the later exchange.

  2. 02 The human identifies a recoverable defect

    A reviewer can determine the defect directly or reasonably infer it from context available before the human feedback.
